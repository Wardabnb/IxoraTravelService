Our Destinations

Tunisia

Tunisia, located in North Africa, is surrounded by Algeria to the west, Libya to the southeast, and the Mediterranean Sea to the north and east. Its geography boasts a diverse landscape, incorporating coastal plains, fertile valleys, and the Atlas Mountains in the north.

Learn More

Turkey

Turkey, located at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, is bordered by eight countries and surrounded by three seas: the Mediterranean Sea to the south, the Aegean Sea to the west, and the Black Sea to the north. Its geographical diversity encompasses majestic mountains, expansive plains, and captivating coastlines.

Learn More

Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia, occupying the majority of the Arabian Peninsula, is flanked by Jordan, Iraq, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, the United Arab Emirates, Oman, and Yemen. Its defining feature is its expansive desert terrain, notably the Rub' al Khali (Empty Quarter), the largest continuous sand desert globally.

Learn More

China

China, positioned in East Asia, holds the distinction of being the world's fourth-largest country by land area. Its extensive borders connect with 14 neighboring nations, including Russia, India, and Vietnam. The country's diverse terrain features towering mountain ranges, expansive plains, vast deserts, and extensive coastlines.

Learn More

Algeria

Algeria's geography is diverse, featuring the Atlas Mountains in the north, the Saharan Atlas in the central region, and the expansive Sahara Desert covering much of the south. The northern coastal region enjoys a Mediterranean climate, while the interior experiences arid and desert climates.

Learn More
